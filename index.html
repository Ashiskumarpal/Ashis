About me

I am a motivated and skilled engineer with a diverse background in industrial production, data analysis, and systems engineering. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Industrial and Production Engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology, followed by three and a half years of experience in various manufacturing roles, including Production Planning Executive, Master Production Scheduler, and Production Manager.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Science in Civil and Environmental Engineering with a concentration in Data and Systems. My research focuses on urban rail transportation networks, specifically exploring the impact of their interconnectedness on functionality and connectivity under various hazards and compound events. My skillset extends beyond traditional engineering, encompassing data analysis, machine learning, process improvement methodologies like Lean systems and sustainability, and web development using HTML, CSS, JS and XML. I am comfortable working with data-intensive projects and am eager to leverage my diverse skillset to contribute to innovative and impactful solutions.
